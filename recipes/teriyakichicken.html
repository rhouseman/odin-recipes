Baked Teriyaki Chicken

This recipe will have your family screaming for more!

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Soy Sauce
  • Sugar
  • Vinegar
  • Cornstarch and cold water
  • Minced garlic
  • Seasonings

Steps

  1. Set aside chicken.
  2. Combine rest of ingredients in a small saucepan over low heat.
  3. Place chicken in baking dish.
  4. Brush both side with the sauce.
  5. Bake in preheated oven for 30 minutes.
  6. Flip chicken and brush with sauce every 10 minutes until no longer pink and juices run clear.
  7. Serve hot and enjoy!
